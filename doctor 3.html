About Dr. Aditya Dave

Dr. Aditya Dave is a dedicated Ayurvedic practitioner committed to restoring health through the timeless principles of Ayurveda. With years of clinical experience, he believes that true healing begins when we align our lifestyle with the laws of nature. His approach is not merely to treat symptoms but to identify and eliminate the root cause of ailments by balancing the body’s vital energies.

At our center, we specialize in Nadi Pariksha (Pulse Diagnosis), an ancient diagnostic tool that reveals the physical and emotional status of an individual. By understanding your unique Prakriti (Body Constitution), Dr. Dave crafts personalized wellness protocols that include herbal formulations, dietary adjustments, and mindfulness practices tailored specifically to your genetic makeup.

Beyond general consultation, Dr. Dave is a proponent of Panchakarma—the five-fold purification process. He has successfully helped patients manage chronic conditions such as arthritis, digestive disorders, skin ailments, and stress-related issues. Our mission is to empower you with the knowledge and natural tools needed to achieve a state of Svastha—optimal health and mental clarity.
